## Task Tracking	

We are using [Kanbanflow.com](http://Kanbanflow.com). When a new module is started we will divide it by tasks, each task will be assigned to one or more team members and will be given a task on the kanban board. The tasks will start in the To-Do column and as we plan due dates of each task and as tasks are being worked or completed they are moved across the board.
